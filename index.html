About Sonya

Life is good - but it's better at the lake! Sonya has been helping buyers and sellers of lake front properties in Gladwin & Midland County for over 15 years! She was born and raised in the community and has enjoyed the area lakes all her life. Whether it is at Pontoon Alley on Wixom Lake, one of the popular sand bars at Secord Lake or enjoying the 4th of July boat parade on Wiggins Lake Sonya knows the lakes well and the lake front real estate market even better!

Sonya specializes in the purchase and sale of lake front property in Gladwin & Midland County and services Sanford Lake, Wixom Lake, Secord Lake, Smallwood Lake, Wiggins Lake, Pratt Lake, the lakes in Sugar Springs and all bodies of water in between!

Sonya holds her Associate Brokers license and several additional designations including ABR, GRI and SFR and is a member of MAR and NAR. She is consistently the top sales agent on Wixom Lake and is always a top producing agent within the entire county! Her professionalism, enthusiasm and easy nature allow your transaction to be an uncomplicated and enjoyable process!
